Artsy Column Recreation

I dig how Artsy works their fixed columns and want to work on recreating this in order to refine my knowledge of the jQuery used to make it happen.

What this page does...

This page is simply set up to give one of the many articles below (with class 'chimp') the jQuery rigging to (1) recognize when the article is at the top of the browser window, (2) comfortably within the window, and (3) below the window. Getting this foundation in place is key before some of the more granular effects can be implemented.
