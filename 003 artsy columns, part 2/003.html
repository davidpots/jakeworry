Artsy Column Recreation

I dig how Artsy works their fixed columns and want to work on recreating this in order to refine my knowledge of the jQuery used to make it happen.

What this page does...

This page takes 002 and adds some interaction details to the target article (the third one down the page). Specifically, the side column will fix itself to the top of the screen when scrolling down, and the bottom of the screen when scrolling up. This effect is not yet implemented on all 'article' items on this page, yet -- that will be in the future. I wanted to prove the concept in a single item, first.

To view the effect, scroll down and check out the 3rd article.
